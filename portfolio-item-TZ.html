Introducing TrackZone

Strive For progress

  • Swift
  • Xcode
  • Firebase

TrackZone uses the sensors within a userâ€™s Phone to track their, average pace, distance and steps. TrackZone includes custom experiences with personalised profiles through signing up. Users can store workouts and progress onto their device. Users of TrackZone also have the ability to calculate their BMI through the app if they wish. Through this experience, users can track their progress and abilities with ease and can strive for progress not perfection.

Research was conducted into the general fitness app market where demographic, features and verification were looked at. I also did research into platforms and software that could be used to best create the mobile app. From this research the requirements of the app were made and analysed and UML designs were made to break down the app

After this, various prototypes of the application were made. First a paper prototype then a digital prototype, each with increasing fidelity. Following this the final IOS app was create using swift on Xcode.
