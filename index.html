Software Development

As a full stack software developer i enjoy developing different types of software both in my spare time and professionally. I am proficient in the use of many languages and platforms which have helped me develop many websites and applications over the past few years.

UX/UI Design

I use knowledge of UX to gain deeper understandings of how users think as well as how they would best interact with various aspects of a given software. Techniques such as Personas, Customer journey maps, mind mapping and user interview are utilised. I then use that information to design, prototype and create efficient systems for users to operate through UI design.

Data Analytics

Can analyse raw data and sort it using various software such as excel, and tableau to form a strong understanding of data and form necessary conclusions which can be used to optimize processes or increase the overall efficiency of a business or system. With this data, i can also construct visualisations in the form of interactive dashboards and infographics to better communicate data trends to users
